# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Give actionable, technical feedback.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting comments, or inflammatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Any conduct that creates an unsafe environment.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, open a private security advisory or contact the maintainers through project support channels.

All reports will be reviewed and handled as promptly and fairly as possible.
